# 《逃离贫困线》应用需求文档

*Escape the Poverty Line App Requirements Document*

------

## 一、概述 | Overview

**应用名称**：逃离贫困线（又名：逃离斩杀）
**App Name**: Escape the Poverty Line (Also Known As: Escape the Kill Line)

**核心宗旨**：帮助底层与中层人群清晰认知自身财务风险，通过预警、资源整合与习惯培养，提升生存与抗风险能力。
**Core Purpose**: To help low- and middle-income individuals clearly understand their financial risks, improve survival and resilience through early warnings, resource integration, and habit formation.

**背景**：在缺乏存款的情况下，突发支出可能导致流浪或死亡。本应用旨在通过量化风险、提供政策资源、引导储蓄，帮助用户“逃离斩杀线”。
**Background**: Unexpected expenses can lead to homelessness or death without savings. This app aims to help users "escape the kill line" by quantifying risks, providing policy resources, and encouraging savings.

------

## 二、核心功能 | Core Features

### 第一步：斩杀线预警系统 | Step 1: Kill Line Early Warning System

**功能描述**：

- 基于用户所在地的法律、税务、基本生活成本，自动计算每月最低生存支出。
- 对比用户收入，实时显示结余金额与“斩杀线距离”（如：结余100美元以下即危险）。
- 当结余低于阈值时，推送预警提示，并说明可能引发的风险（如突发医疗、房租上涨等）。

**Feature Description**:

- Automatically calculates minimum monthly survival expenses based on local laws, taxes, and basic living costs.
- Compares with user income to show remaining balance and "distance to kill line" (e.g., less than $100 remaining is dangerous).
- Sends alerts when balance is below threshold, explaining potential risks (e.g., medical emergencies, rent increases).

------

### 第二步：政策与资源整合 | Step 2: Policy & Resource Integration

**功能描述**：

- 聚合当地优惠政策（如消费券、食品补助、税费减免、免费医疗资源等）。
- 根据用户画像（收入、家庭规模、地区）推送个性化生存资源。
- 支持用户提交政策反馈，形成“用户互助式信息更新”。

**Feature Description**:

- Aggregates local support policies (e.g., coupons, food assistance, tax relief, free medical resources).
- Pushes personalized survival resources based on user profile (income, family size, location).
- Allows users to submit policy updates, creating a "crowdsourced information system."

------

### 第三步：智能账单与储蓄引导 | Step 3: Smart Budgeting & Savings Guidance

**功能描述**：

- 自动分类账单支出（必要 vs 非必要），生成月度消费报告。
- 设置“虚拟斩杀线”挑战，鼓励用户通过削减非必要支出进行储蓄。
- 提供渐进式储蓄目标与行为激励（如：“本周少喝咖啡，储蓄5美元”）。

**Feature Description**:

- Automatically categorizes expenses (essential vs. non-essential) and generates monthly spending reports.
- Creates "virtual kill line" challenges to encourage saving by reducing non-essential spending.
- Provides gradual savings goals and behavioral incentives (e.g., "Skip coffee this week, save $5").

------

## 三、目标用户 | Target Users

### 1. 底层人员 | Low-Income Group

- **需求**：获取免费食物、补贴券、临时住宿等紧急生存资源。
- **Needs**: Access to free food, subsidy vouchers, temporary shelter, and other emergency resources.

### 2. 中层人员 | Middle-Income Group

- **需求**：清晰了解自身财务安全边际，预防因政策变动或突发风险滑落底层。
- **Needs**: Clear understanding of financial safety margins to prevent falling into poverty due to policy changes or emergencies.

------

## 四、关键挑战与解决方案 | Key Challenges & Solutions

| 挑战                     | 解决方案                                       |
| :----------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| **政策信息更新不及时**   | 用户反馈机制 + 合作地方政府/NGO 提供数据       |
| **储蓄习惯难以培养**     | 游戏化挑战 + 渐进目标 + 社会激励（如家庭小组） |
| “斩杀线”概念可能引发焦虑 | 用“安全边际”等中性表述，强化解决方案引导       |
| 地区差异大（法律、物价） | 建立本地化数据库，允许用户手动调整参数         |

## 五、商业模式 | Business Model

- 一次性付费下载（如1美元）或“按需付费”（高级预警功能）。
- 免费基础版 + 付费高级资源包（如深度政策分析、个性化规划）。
- 与政府、社会福利机构合作，获取补贴或采购。

**One-time purchase (e.g., $1) or "pay-for-features" model (advanced alerts).
Free basic version + paid premium packs (e.g., policy analysis, personalized planning).
Partnerships with governments/NGOs for subsidies or procurement.**

## 七、附录 | Appendix

- 隐私保护：用户财务数据仅本地存储，可选匿名上传用于政策分析。
- 可持续性：建立用户社群，鼓励共享生存策略与本地信息。
- 长期愿景：从“逃离斩杀”到“走向稳定”，逐步扩展至职业培训、心理健康支持模块。

**Privacy Protection**: Financial data stored locally only; optional anonymous upload for policy analysis.
**Sustainability**: Build user communities for sharing survival strategies and local information.
**Long-term Vision**: From "escaping the kill line" to "achieving stability," expanding to job training and mental health support modules.
